# Groups

Groups are reusable sets of users you share trips with. Instead of sharing a
trip with people one by one, share it with a group and every member gets access.

## Managing groups

From **Groups** in the sidebar:

| Action | How |
| --- | --- |
| Create | Enter a group name and save. |
| Add member | Enter the user's email address. They must already have a Roamarr account. |
| Remove member | Remove a member from the group. |
| Delete group | Deletes the group; shared trips keep their existing members. |

Only the group owner can add or remove members and delete the group.

## Sharing with a group

When sharing a trip, choose **Share with group** and pick a group. Members gain
the access level you choose (view or edit). See [Sharing](./sharing.md) for the
full sharing model.

## Privacy

Group membership is visible only to the owner. Other members do not see a list of
everyone in the group unless they also have access to a shared trip with those
members.
